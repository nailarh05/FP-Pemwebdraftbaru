üöÄ How to Test Your Unjumble Game

  1. Make sure both servers are running:
    Frontend: npm run dev
    Backend: bun run start:dev
  2. Open your browser and go to http://localhost:3000 (or the port shown in your terminal)
  3. Login to your account
  4. Click "New Game" button
  5. Select "Unjumble" template card
  6. Fill in the form:
    • Title: e.g., "English Grammar Practice"
    • Description: e.g., "Learn sentence structure"
    • Upload a thumbnail image
    • Add sentences (e.g., "Can she play the violin ?")
    • Configure settings
  7. Click "Publish" button
  8. Your game will appear in My Projects
  9. Click "Play" to test the game
  10. Arrange the shuffled words to form correct sentences!
